Kiyo and Yvng Peso

I recently created a song cover art just for fun, inspired by Kiyo's amazing music video and my love for his work. As a frequent listener of his music in the Philippines, I've been following his career for years and it's been incredible to see him rise to the top of the local hip-hop scene. It's amazing how music and art can come together to create something truly special, and I'm grateful to be able to express my admiration for Kiyo's work in my own way through this cover art.

The lyrics of the song describe the struggle of being unable to sleep and the toll it takes on one's mind and body. Kiyo sings about his eyes that can't rest, his attempts to force himself to sleep, and the loneliness that comes with staying up late at night. The repetition of the word "puyat", which means "sleep-deprived" in Tagalog, creates a sense of monotony and frustration, emphasizing the feeling of being trapped in a cycle of insomnia.
