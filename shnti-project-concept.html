Shnti Element

Despite the challenges of the pandemic, SHNTI has experienced a surge in popularity and has quickly become one of the most exciting names in the local underground hip hop scene. Her career trajectory is impressive, and like many up-and-coming rappers and producers of the Soundcloud era, she started as a personal project that she uploaded on the indie-favorite streaming platform.

Thanks to her unique sound and hard work, she has gained a steady following, particularly over the last two years. In addition to her talent as an up-and-coming Filipina rapper, SHNTI has also caught my attention with her music. I particularly enjoy one of her songs and that I added all her songs to my playlist. From the first time I heard her music, I was drawn to the catchy beat, smooth flow, and meaningful lyrics.
